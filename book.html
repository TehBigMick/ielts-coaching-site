Before you book

  • Lessons are 60 minutes unless stated otherwise

  • Please book only if you are Band 6–8 level
  • Be prepared to practise and complete homework

If you’re unsure whether lessons are right for you, start with the free diagnostic.

What happens next

  1. Choose a time that suits your time zone
  2. Complete payment (if required)
  3. Receive confirmation + meeting link
  4. Prepare any writing or speaking samples

All lessons are online. Please join on time — late arrivals reduce lesson time.
