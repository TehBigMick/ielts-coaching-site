About

This isn’t a language school and it isn’t general English tuition. I work specifically on IELTS score movement — mainly Writing and Speaking.

What I actually do

I help IELTS candidates who are stuck — usually at Band 6–6.5 — understand why their score is not improving and what the examiners are penalising.

The focus is simple:

  • Identify the exact band-limiting issues
  • Fix them using the official band descriptors
  • Practise only what will move the score

No generic advice. No busywork. No “study harder”.

What I don’t do

Being clear about this saves everyone time.

  • General English or conversation classes
  • Beginner or foundation-level English
  • Guaranteed band scores (they don’t exist)
  • Template memorisation or model answer copying

If you need foundation English first, I’ll tell you honestly.

Experience & background

Only what’s relevant to IELTS.

IELTS focus

Several years working specifically with IELTS candidates, primarily on Writing Task 2, Writing Task 1, and Speaking Parts 2–3.

Score movement

Most successful students improve between +0.5 and +1.0 bands when they practise consistently and apply feedback properly.

Method

Feedback is always linked to the band descriptors, not vague impressions or personal preferences.

How this differs from schools and platforms

This is usually why students come here after trying other options.

Schools & platforms

  • Mixed-level classes
  • Fixed curriculum regardless of your weak points
  • Generic feedback to save time
  • Progress measured by “finishing the book”

This coaching

  • One-to-one, score-focused work
  • Lessons planned around your actual weaknesses
  • Specific corrections and rewrite guidance
  • Progress measured by band movement

Who I work best with

Being selective improves results.

  • Band 6–8 IELTS candidates
  • University, migration, or re-sit deadlines
  • Students willing to rewrite and practise between lessons
  • People who want honest feedback, not reassurance
