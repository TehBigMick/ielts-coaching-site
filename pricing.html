Policies

Clear rules keep everything simple and fair.

Rescheduling

  • Reschedule with at least 24 hours’ notice.
  • Late cancellations may be charged as the lesson, because the slot cannot be refilled.
  • If I need to reschedule, you’ll get the earliest alternative slot.

Refunds

  • Trial lessons are refundable only if cancelled with 24 hours’ notice.
  • Packages are refundable only for unused lessons (minus any discount already applied).
  • Writing correction is non-refundable once marking has started.

You can adjust these policies to match your real availability. Just don’t be vague — vague policies create disputes.
